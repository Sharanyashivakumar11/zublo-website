Wireframe Landing Page for Ruthuparna Sharma's 5 A.M. Workshop

Challenge

Life coach Ruthuparna Sharma was launching a 5 a.m. wake-up workshop challenge and needed a simple website presence for a short-term campaign. The request was specifically for a single landing page wireframe that could communicate the workshop clearly and drive sign-ups quickly.

The key challenge was creating something that felt fast to consume, while still building enough trust for visitors to commit to an early-morning program.

Solution

We designed a clean, conversion-focused wireframe landing page with:

  • • Bold hero headline + clear CTA to explain the 5 a.m. challenge instantly
  • • Client logo strip showcasing companies Ruthuparna has worked with for immediate credibility
  • • "Why you should attend" section highlighting key benefits in a simple, scannable way
  • • Testimonial-style trust blocks to reinforce real outcomes and confidence
  • • Minimal email signup to grow a community beyond the workshop launch
